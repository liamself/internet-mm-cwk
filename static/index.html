Welcome to the Bishee Barnabee Hotel!

Situated in the historic city of Norwich, next to the Norfolk Broads, the Bishee Barnabee Hotel offers you a holiday full of possibility. Whether you’re here to take in culture at Norwich Castle or the Sainsbury Centre for the Visual Arts, experience nature on the Broads, visit the Norwich Research Park, or simply indulge in some retail therapy in the Norwich Lanes, we’re the perfect place to stay.

The Bishee Barnabee Hotel has 32 beautiful rooms, a restaurant, bar, gym, and conference facilities. We’re easily reached from the city centre, train station, and Norwich International Airport.

We can’t wait to welcome you.
