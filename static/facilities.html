Facilities

The Bishee Barnabee Hotel boasts numerous facilities which are available to all guests.

For hungry guests, we have a restaurant available to cater for breakfast, lunch, and dinner, providing a range of delicious hot food guaranteed to satisfy all tastes. From midday onwards our bar is also open, serving a wide range of both alcoholic and non-alcoholic drinks, as well as a selection of snacks. Room service is also available for guests who fancy dining in the comfort of their own rooms.

In the hotel guests can also find a state-of-the-art gym which is open 24-7.

For guests hosting functions and conferences, we also have 3 conference rooms available which can cater to parties of between 20 and 60 people. Whether you need to host an important meeting, a family reunion, or any other event our function rooms can provide the perfect location. We are located for easy travel from both national and international locations, and with on-site rooms and catering, we can truly provide for any event.
